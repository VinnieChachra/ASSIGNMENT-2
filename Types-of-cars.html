Types Of Cars

SUV (Sport Utility Vehicle): An SUV is a car that is designed for off-road capabilities and provides more space and versatility than a traditional car. SUVs are available in a variety of sizes, from compact to full-size, and can be configured with two or four-wheel drive.

Hatchback: A hatchback is a car that has a rear door that opens upwards, creating a large storage area. Hatchbacks are often smaller and more compact than sedans, making them a popular choice for city driving.

Sedan: A sedan is a traditional four-door car that is designed for daily transportation. They usually have a trunk for storage and are available in a variety of sizes and styles.

Coupe: A coupe is a two-door car that is designed for sportier driving. They typically have a lower and sleeker profile than sedans and often feature more powerful engines.

Convertible: A convertible is a car that has a retractable roof, allowing the driver and passengers to enjoy open-air driving. Convertibles are available in a variety of styles, including hardtop and soft-top.
